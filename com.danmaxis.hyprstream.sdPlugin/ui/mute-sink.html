Tap to toggle the default audio sink mute.

The icon shows the current volume as a percentage and turns red when muted. State mirrors changes made anywhere — XF86 keys, your panel volume slider, or another app's mute call. Polled at 500 ms intervals while visible.

Targets @DEFAULT_AUDIO_SINK@. To change which output is the default sink, use pavucontrol or wpctl set-default ….

Pair this with two Volume Step keys (one with delta +5, another with -5) for a complete deck-side audio control: rocker for level, a single key for mute.
Requires: wpctl (Arch: sudo pacman -S wireplumber).
