Unlike a plain readout, this key alerts: it stays calm until the metric breaches your threshold, then blinks a red ALERT so a problem is impossible to miss at a glance. Defaults: CPU/RAM ≥ 90%, temp ≥ 85°C, battery ≤ 15%.

Reads /proc/stat, /proc/meminfo, /sys/class/thermal, /sys/class/power_supply. No Hyprland required.
